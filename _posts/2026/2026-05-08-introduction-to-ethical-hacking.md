## Introduction
Ethical hacking involves identifying and fixing vulnerabilities in systems to enhance security. It plays a crucial role in protecting organizations from cyber threats.

## What is Ethical Hacking?
Ethical hacking is the practice of testing systems for security weaknesses with permission. Ethical hackers use the same techniques as malicious hackers but with the goal of improving security.

### Types of Ethical Hacking
1. **Web Application Testing**: Identifying vulnerabilities in web applications.
2. **Network Testing**: Assessing the security of network infrastructure.
3. **Social Engineering**: Testing human vulnerabilities.

### Tools for Ethical Hacking
- **Nmap**: Network scanning.
- **Metasploit**: Penetration testing.
- **Wireshark**: Network analysis.

## Conclusion
Ethical hacking is essential for maintaining robust cybersecurity. By proactively identifying and addressing vulnerabilities, organizations can protect their assets and data.
