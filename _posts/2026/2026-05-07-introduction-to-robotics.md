## Introduction
Robotics is an interdisciplinary field that combines engineering, computer science, and AI to design and build robots.

## What is Robotics?
Robotics involves creating machines that can perform tasks autonomously or with human guidance. Robots are used in various industries, from manufacturing to healthcare.

### Key Components of Robotics
1. **Sensors**: Collecting data from the environment.
2. **Actuators**: Enabling movement and interaction.
3. **Control Systems**: Programming robots to perform tasks.

### Applications of Robotics
- **Manufacturing**: Automating production lines.
- **Healthcare**: Assisting in surgeries and patient care.
- **Exploration**: Conducting missions in space and underwater.

## Conclusion
Robotics is transforming industries by enhancing efficiency and innovation. As technology advances, robots will play an increasingly important role in our lives.
